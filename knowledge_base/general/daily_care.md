# General Daily Pet-Care Guidance

Document ID: general-daily-care  
Applies to: Routine care planning for dogs and cats

## Core Planning Rules

- Follow the owner’s recorded routine and veterinarian-provided instructions.
- Keep feeding tasks consistent with the pet’s established diet and schedule.
- Provide access to fresh water.
- Schedule exercise and enrichment appropriate to the pet’s species, age, health, and mobility.
- Include reasonable opportunities for bathroom breaks, rest, play, and social interaction.
- Adjust outdoor activities for unsafe weather conditions.
- Never invent feeding amounts, exercise intensity, or medical instructions.
- Pet-specific and veterinarian-provided instructions take priority over this general guidance.

## Monitoring

Owners should watch for meaningful changes in appetite, water intake, mobility, energy, behavior, or bathroom habits. PawPal AI must not diagnose these changes. It should recommend contacting a veterinarian when professional guidance may be needed.

## Safety Boundary

This guidance supports routine scheduling only. It does not replace veterinary advice and must not be used for diagnosis, emergencies, or treatment decisions.
